small spaces

Sleeping Loft

A loft kit from Tecrostar allowed us to install a double bed over our office desks. Easy to put together, the kit resembles giant Meccano.

source

Soaking Tub

Measuring just 750 by 1050cm, the Omnitub deep soaking tub was nicknamed 'the cowboy bath' by our builder. When plumbed up it will also serve as a shower tray.

source

Folding Doors

Three sets of folding door kits from PC Henderson give us huge flexibility on the ground floor. We’ve used kits with (Pacer) and without (Roomflex) bottom channels.

source

Pantry

As one door gets closed up another opens… or maybe your carpenter shelves it out for a pantry:) A bulk order of jars from Alpack and a Dymo label maker from Amazon completed the look.

source

kitchen

Previous Next

Although minimal, our kitchen is super-efficient – unloading the dishwasher takes no more than two steps. The cabinets, shelf and pantry are tulip wood with an almost clear satin varnish. Awkward changes in ceiling height meant we opted for no upper cabinets. The quartz worktop was a complete splurge but it’s both beautiful and practical.







favourites

During the renovation every free moment was spent online searching for a light fitting or shelf bracket. Here are a few of the online retailers and brands that made our favourites list.

red bubble

Affordable art from independent artists. You can loose hours trawling through this site but beware of colour differences between screen and actual print.

source

all handles & pulls

Exceptional quality and design from a Swedish supplier. If you’re looking for something a little different, this site has a lot of Nordic brands.

source

house doctor

Modern Danish brand House Doctor produce a huge range of home accessories with a huge amount of style. Royal Design is the best online outlet we’ve found.

source

vita copenhagen

As well as Vita Copenhagen’s Acorn lights in the kitchen, we have their goose feather EOS in a bedroom. Watch out for their pendant fittings which are sold separately, some are very plastic.

source

best tile

A great selection of Moroccan cement tiles that can be purchased online and in small quantities.

source

ikea

Whether you love it or loath it, where else can you get a hook that looks like a dogs tail?
